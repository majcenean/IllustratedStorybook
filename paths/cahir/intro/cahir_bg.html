Before her stood a knight with a great sword, in a black cloak and helmet decorated with the wings of a bird of prey.
He looked even more wretched and vulnerable than when Geralt had seen him on his knees, wounded, trembling with fear in a pool of blood.

He also looked much younger. The witcher did not give him more than twenty-five years.
