SOMETHING MORE.
"Who is this child, Wolf? Who is this girl?"

"She's our destiny."
"How do you know Ciri would want to go with me? Because of some old prophecies?"

"No. Because she only fell asleep after you cuddled her. Because she mutters your name and searches for your hand in her sleep."

"Enough," Geralt got up, "because I'm liable to get emotional."
The mousy-haired girl in a grey dress. And the white-haired Witcher with a sword on his back, all dressed in black leather, gleaming with silver.

"Geralt!" the little girl repeated, clinging to the Witcher's chest. "You found me! I knew you would! It's like they said! Am I your destiny? Say it! Am I your destiny?"
"You're more than that, Ciri. Much more."
