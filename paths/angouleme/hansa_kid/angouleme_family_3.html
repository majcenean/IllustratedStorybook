The door slammed open. Geralt rushed into the kitchen.

"Come on, company, get packed," he announced without unnecessary introductions.
"We're riding out! In an hour, and not a moment longer."

That was enough. It was as though they'd been waiting for this news for a long time, as they they'd been in readiness for a long time.

"Angoulême, why are you making faces?"
"Geralt, I—"
"Don't worry, I'm not leaving you.
I've changed my mind. You need looking after, my girl, I can't let you out of my sight. To horse, I said, get packed and fasten on your saddlebags."
"Without fail, Geralt!" yelled Angouleme.
"At fucking last!"
