“We had our ups and downs, won some, lost some, we gave and took some beatings.
Full bellies, empty bellies.

Ha, more often empty.

I’ve eaten everything that crawls, anything you can fucking catch.
And things that fly? I even ate a child’s kite once, because it was made of flour and water paste.”
She fell silent, then distractedly messed up her flaxen hair.
“What’s past is past. I’ll just say this:

No one who escaped with me from the orphanage is still alive.”
