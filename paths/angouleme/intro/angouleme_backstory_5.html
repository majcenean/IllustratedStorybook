“Prig! Very well, I’ll get to the point, because I don’t feel like talking.
We found knives in the orphanage kitchen. We made some excellent clubs from the turned up legs of an oaken chair.

All we needed was horses and coin, so we waited for two perverts, regular customers, old buggers of, ugh, at least forty.
They came, sat down, sipped wine…
but they didn’t get their oats that day!”
“Angoulême.”
