“You didn’t expect this when we set off, did you?
“When you let me join the company? You thought: “So What if she’s a peasant; a foolish, country wench?” You let me join. “I won’t be able to talk to her about brainy things on the road,” you thought, “but she might come in useful. She’s a healthy, sturdy lass. She shoots a straight arrow.”
“And it turns out she’s no use, just a hindrance. A millstone.”
“A typical bloody woman!”
