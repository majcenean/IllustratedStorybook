The Witcher universe was created by Andrzej Sapkowski, a contemporary Polish fantasy author.

The text for this project was taken from the series over the 8 books in The Witcher series:

  • The Last Wish (trans. Danusia Stok),
  • The Sword of Destiny (trans. David French),
  • Blood of Elves (trans. Danusia Stok),
  • Time of Contempt (trans. David French),
  • Baptism of Fire (trans. David French),
  • The Tower of the Swallow (trans. David French),
  • Lady of the Lake (trans. David French), and
  • Season of Storms (trans. David French).

The saga addresses the witcher Geralt and others' reactions to the raising and later disappearance of a very special girl, Cirilla "Ciri" of Cintra, who is Geralt's daughter by bonds of destiny.

In seeking to rescue his daughter, Geralt finds himself accompanied by a group of comrades willing to share in his journey. Their resemblance of a traditional RPG team of heroes [with two swordsmen, a ranger, a healer, and a rogue] inspired this concept.

This project serves as a telling of their story reimagined in the medium of web design, an interactive storybook woven from curated scenes and quotes from the series.
